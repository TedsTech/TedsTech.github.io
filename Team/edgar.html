Edgar J. Zavala Background

Edgar Zavala was born in Moroleon, GTO, Mexico on October 9. He immigrated to the United States at the age of 3. When Edgar came to the United States he moved to Chicago. In Chicago he has been a resident of La Villita all of his life. La Villita is a rapid growing, all latino community on the lower west side of Chicago. Growing up he has been around an all latino neighborhood. Edgar was exposed to crime, violence, and gang activity and was set to make a change for his community.

Edgar is a graduate of Cristo Rey Jesuit High School in Chicago, and there I was exposed to a professional business environment at an early age. He interned at Katten Muchin Rosenman LLP through Cristo Reyâ€™s Corporate Work Study Program.

At KMR Edgar learned to balance both school and work to learn responsibilities and great work ethic. After high school, Edgar studied at Concordia University Chicago.

He then realized I wanted to go into the IT field, and became a student at Year Up Chicago, and continues to work part-time at KMR in the Office Services department. At Year Up Edgar is looking to enrich his learning with Software, Hardware and Web Development.
